Most Waited of 2023

Hogwarts Legacy

Synopsis

Players will experience the lives of students at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1800s as they embark on a perilous journey to uncover a hidden truth of the wizarding world. They will develop their magical skills by mastering spells, brewing potions, taming magical animals and more.

Dead Space Remake

Synopsis

Isaac Clarke is an engineer with a mission to repair a huge mining ship, but discovers that the crew has been slaughtered and his beloved is lost somewhere. Alone, he will have to unravel the horrifying mystery of what happened on board.

Forspoken

Synopsis

Forspoken tells the journey of Frey, a young New Yorker brought to the beautiful and cruel Athia. Frey must use her newfound magical abilities to explore and face monstrous creatures in search of a way back home.

Suicide Squad: Kill the Justice League

Synopsis

Featuring a narrative set in the expansive open-world city of Metropolis, Suicide Squad: Kill the Justice League pits four DC Super Villains against an invading force from another planet and against DC Super Heroes, who now aim to destroy the city they once swore to protect.

Wild Hearts

Synopsis

Master ancient technologies to hunt giant beasts. WILD HEARTS™ innovates the hunting genre with ancient technologies that make it possible to fight giant beasts transformed by nature. Hunt on your own or with your friends.

Resident Evil 4 Remake

Synopsis

The story of Resident Evil 4 follows US government special agent Leon S. Kennedy, who is sent on a mission to rescue Ashley Graham, daughter of the US president, who has been kidnapped by a macabre cult.

Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom

Synopsis

After Calamity Ganon's defeat, Link frees Zelda and they both venture inside Hyrule Castle. There they find a mummified body that resembles that of Ganondorf and, then, an evil energy awakens and separates the hero and the princess.

Final Fantasy XVI

Synopsis

The war rages on, and Valisthea enters an age of twilight. Where will fate lead the Dominants? And what will the future of the kingdom look like after this bitter conflict ends? There are still many unanswered questions and many truths to be revealed.

Star Wars Jedi: Survivor

Synopsis

Continue Cal's journey. No longer a Padawan, Cal became a powerful Jedi Knight. The Dark Times are approaching. Surrounded by enemies new and familiar, Cal will have to decide how far he's willing to go to save those closest to him.

Marvel’s Spider-Man 2

Synopsis

The game continues the adventures of Peter Parker and Miles Morales, with the two acting together to defend New York from new threats. One of the villains that the duo will have to face is Venom, which means that the story will involve the Symbiotes, and perhaps it will be possible to see the hero's famous black outfit in a ction.
