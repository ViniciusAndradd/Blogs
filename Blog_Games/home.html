Brazil Game Awards: See list of all award winners

The Brazil Game Awards already have the results of the 2022 winners. Among all the games, Elden Ring, from FromSoftware, won the main prize when it was chosen as the 2022 Game of the Year. The independent jury, made up of different vehicles, content producers and video game critics, chose the best games of the year in different categories. In addition to the newest soulslike from FromSoftware, God of War Ragnarök and the Brazilian Phobia – St. Dinfna Hotel also stood out.

PSVR 2 has over 30 titles confirmed for launch

Sony officially confirmed the PlayStation VR2 launch window games until March 2023. There are 13 new titles that were not announced for the platform before, among them is Tetris Effect: Connected and Rez Infinite. As detailed on PlayStation.Blog , PlayStation VR2 will launch on February 22, 2023, and over 30 games will be available on the platform before the end of March, if nothing changes.
